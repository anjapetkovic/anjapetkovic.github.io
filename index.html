Anja Petković Komel

Anja Petković Komel

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Vienna University of Technology.
I did my PhD at Faculty of mathematics and physics, University of Ljubljana. The title of my PhD thesis is Meta-analysis of type theories with an application to the design of formal proofs. My mentor was Andrej Bauer. I am interested in type theory, development of proof assistants and logic.

Previously I have worked with Alex Simpson on my master thesis (source code) on computing fixed points of monotone piecewise linear functions.

Research interests

Type Theory, Homotopy Type Theory, Logic.
